Words that are easy to confuse in TOPIK

🧍다의어Polysemy

맡다 🔊

1get (a seat)

예시험 기간이라서 도서관 자리 맡기가 쉽지 않다. 🔊

It is really hard to get a seat in the library since it is the mid-term season.

2smell

예감기에 걸려서 냄새를 잘 맡을 수가 없어요. 🔊

I cannot smell since I have a cold.

3care

예아이를 맡아 줄 시설이 많아지면 좋겠다. 🔊

I wish there are many more facilities that provide daycare services.
